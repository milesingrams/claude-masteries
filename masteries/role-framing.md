## Chip

Who should Claude be for this?

## Triggers

- User is asking for expert-level help in a specific domain
- User's task would benefit from a particular perspective
- User hasn't specified what kind of expertise they need
- Response could vary significantly based on the framing
- User is asking for advice, feedback, or analysis

## Satisfaction

- User tells Claude what role, expertise, or perspective to adopt
- User frames the request with a specific lens
- Phrases: "as a", "from the perspective of", "like you're a", "imagine you're", "as an expert in", "with the mindset of"

## Detail

The same question yields different answers depending on who's answering. Asking Claude to respond "as a senior engineer" vs "as a product manager" vs "as a skeptical customer" shapes what it prioritizes, notices, and recommends. The frame is part of the prompt.
